Mochi Waffles

Description

This recipe will let you make very tasty mochi waffles. Add toppings like strawberries, blueberries, chocolate, or maple syrup for best results!

Ingredients

  • 5/4 cup Mochiko Flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a medium mixing bowl add milk, egg, and vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined. Set aside.
  2. In another medium mixing bowl add mochiko flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry. Whisk until just combined.
  4. Preheat waffle iron
  5. Add 1/4 to 1/2 a cup of batter to the waffle iron
  6. Cook for 3-5 minutes
